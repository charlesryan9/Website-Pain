About Meowgi-Do Pest Control

At Meowgi-Do Pest Control, we believe there’s nothing more efficient than whiskers, claws, and a discerning meow when it comes to dealing with uninvited pests. Our team of highly trained cats prowls your property day and night, ensuring every nook and cranny is pest-free. No traps, no poisons—just paws and purrs.
